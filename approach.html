How We Work

The OPNIGMA Process —
Eight Steps to Full Execution.

A disciplined, end-to-end process built for logistics operations. No shortcuts. No generic playbooks. Every step earns the next.

Phase One

Discovery & Strategy.

Before any recommendation, any roadmap, any technology — we listen, assess, and understand. The quality of everything that follows depends on the quality of what we learn here.

01
Discovery

Client Consultation

We start by listening. Before assessments or recommendations, we take time to understand your business — your goals, your pain points, what you've already tried, and what success actually looks like for your team. This conversation shapes everything that follows.

02
Assessment

Operations & Technology Assessment

We assess your business across three dimensions: tools, technology, and people. We examine how work actually gets done — not just what your org chart or software list describes. The highest-impact opportunities are almost always invisible until someone looks at the full picture.

03
Technical Audit

IT Discovery Assessment

A structured deep dive into your IT infrastructure — systems, integrations, security posture, cloud readiness, and the technical debt that's quietly limiting your performance. Documented with precision. Presented without jargon.

04
Strategy

Strategy Development

We translate findings into a prioritized roadmap. Each recommendation is tied to a measurable business outcome and sequenced for realistic execution inside a running operation. You receive clear direction — not a report that gathers dust.

Phase Two

Execution & Delivery.

The strategy is only as good as the execution. This is where we build, deploy, and make the change stick — inside a live operation, without disruption.

05
Alignment

Findings Report & Client Presentation

We present findings directly to your leadership. We walk through what we found, what we recommend, and why it matters. This is a working session — not a formality. Decisions get made here.

06
Planning

Project Mapping

Scope, timeline, milestones, resources, and dependencies — mapped with the operational realities of a running freight business in mind. We plan implementations that don't require shutting down to succeed.

07
Build

Technology Implementation

We build, configure, integrate, and deploy. Carrier API integrations, cloud migrations, automation workflows, TMS implementations — executed by people who have built these systems in live production environments.

08
Adoption

Onboarding & Change Management

Technology only delivers value if your team uses it effectively. We design structured onboarding programs, train staff at every level, and manage the organizational change that makes new systems stick. We measure success by adoption and outcome — not go-live date.

Step Zero

It Starts With a Conversation.

Step one is simple: we listen. No pitch, no proposal, no pressure. Tell us about your operation and what you need. We'll tell you honestly what we can do.
