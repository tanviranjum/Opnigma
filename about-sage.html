About OPNIGMA

We've Lived the Problem
We're Solving.

OPNIGMA was born because the gap between what a system could do and what the operation needed kept widening. We understand both the urgency on a warehouse floor and the logic inside the server stack.

Our work doesn't stop at a slide deck. We build environments that scale and processes that accelerate. This isn't consulting. It's engineered momentum.

Our Story

The Gap We Were Built to Close.

Standard IT consultants understood technology but not the operation. Logistics experts understood the operation but not how to engineer it. The result: expensive systems that didn't fit, and operational wisdom that was never codified.

OPNIGMA was founded to close that gap. We combine deep logistics operations experience with the technical capability to build, integrate, and deploy — in production, inside a running freight business.

This isn't consulting. It's engineered momentum.

How We Operate

Six Values That Drive Everything.
